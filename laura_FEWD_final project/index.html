Buscabulla (Spanish slang for troublemaker) is the solo project of Puerto Rican designer and Brooklyn resident, Raquel Berrios. Heavily influenced by vintage Latin music like salsa gorda, Cuban psych and '80s Argentinian rock, the project combines both electronic and live instrumentation. Her diverse influences make up a hybrid that combines a mellow Caribbean sensibility with subversive wit.
